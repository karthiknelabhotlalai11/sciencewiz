## Full Description (4000 characters max)

📚 **ScienceWiz** is the ultimate offline science companion for CBSE Class 9 and Class 10 students. Everything you need — from the Periodic Table to NCERT experiments — in one beautifully designed app. No internet required. No registration needed.

---

### 🔬 5 POWERFUL MODULES

**⚗️ Periodic Table Explorer**
• 30 key elements with full details
• Atomic number, mass, valency, electronic configuration
• Real-world uses and fun facts for every element
• Filter by category: metals, non-metals, noble gases and more
• Search by name, symbol or atomic number

**⚡ Physics Formula Sheet**
• Chapter-wise formulas: Motion, Force, Gravitation, Work & Energy, Sound, Light, Electricity, Magnetism
• Fully NCERT Class 9 & 10 aligned
• Each formula includes description, SI unit and solved example
• One-tap copy formula to clipboard
• Filter by Class 9 or Class 10

**🧪 Chemical Equation Balancer**
• 15+ NCERT chemical equations with step-by-step balancing
• Covers: combustion, displacement, decomposition, neutralisation reactions
• Shows full working — not just the answer
• Quick-tap examples for common NCERT reactions
• Haber Process, rusting, electrolysis and more

**🫀 Human Body Systems**
• 6 body systems: Digestive, Respiratory, Circulatory, Nervous, Excretory, Endocrine
• Detailed notes on each organ: location, functions, diseases, key facts
• NCERT chapter reference for every organ
• Searchable across all systems

**🔬 Experiment Guide**
• 10 complete NCERT practical experiments
• Step-by-step numbered procedure
• Materials list, observations, result and conclusion
• Precautions clearly listed
• Covers Physics, Chemistry and Biology
